Emergency Phone Call In Australia
These are public emergency service numbers provided by the Australian government. Call them at any time when you feel unsafe.

000 - The general emergency number

131 126 - Poisons Information Centre : 24 hours

131 114 - Life Line : 24 hours

131 638 - Public transport & timetables

131 176 - Accident Towing

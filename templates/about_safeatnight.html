Night-Shift Worker Safety

Safety for night shift workers is a growing concern in Melbourne, and with the current trend in crime rates going up, these night shift workers are at a greater risk.

In this age of technology, there is very little data on areas of concern such as crime rate in different locations and emergency alerts if in danger.

Our site www.safeatnight.cf addresses the above mentioned issues, specifically aimed at night shift workers to utilise in making their lives more secure, as outline below.

  • The specific areas of concern focuses on the crime statistics by area.
  • A safety switch functionality is available for users to send out alerts SMS at a set time.
  • Safety tips for new visitors in Melbourne to get to know this city.
